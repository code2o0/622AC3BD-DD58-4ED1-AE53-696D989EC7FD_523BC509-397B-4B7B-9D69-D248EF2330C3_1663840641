More Usage Methods

1.You can use this App to temporarily monitor the sleep of babies or children, and enable motion detection and configure the notification method. The App will send you a notification according to the notification method you configured when the screen changes.

2.If you are staying in a hotel and you are concerned that strangers may snoop, you can use this app, point the camera at the cat's eye hole on the door, and enable face detection and configure notification methods, if someone is snooping on your room, and when the App detects a face, it will send you a notification according to the notification method you configured.

3.If you are in need of a device similar to a dash cam, this is undoubtedly a very suitable App to temporarily act as your dash cam
